Introduction
Welcome to the wire-by-wire interactive guide.

Instead of a messy picture with all wires at once, we will connect one wire at a time.

Look at the board on the right. You have 4 main parts:
1. Grid Relay (R1) - Senses city power.
2. Gen Timer (T1) - Delays generator start.
3. Grid Contactor (K1) - The heavy switch for city power.
4. Gen Contactor (K2) - The heavy switch for generator power.

Click "Start Wiring" to run the first wire.
