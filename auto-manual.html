Introduction
Welcome to the Advanced Auto/Manual ATS wiring.

We have added:
1. A Selector Switch (3 position): Connects power to Auto or Manual logic.
2. Two Push Buttons: For starting the Grid or Generator manually.
3. Holding Contacts (13/14): To keep the contactors closed after you let go of the push button.
